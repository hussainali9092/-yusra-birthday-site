Happy 19th Birthday

I know this is landing a day late, but I wanted to give you something I actually took my time on — not something rushed out at midnight. You deserve that much and more.

Nineteen is a good look on you. Every year I think you can't possibly become more you, and every year I'm wrong. You've grown into someone kind, funny, a little chaotic in the best way, and endlessly warm to everyone lucky enough to know you — and I get to be one of those people every single day.

I think about how we started more than you'd probably guess. Not the smoothest beginning — I got jealous, I said what I was feeling, and somehow that turned into this. Into us. I've never once regretted saying it out loud that day.

We haven't had a perfectly straight road. There were stretches where things fell apart, time we spent without each other, and I won't pretend that didn't hurt. But every time, without fail, I've found my way back to you — and I don't think that's an accident. I think that's just what it looks like when something is actually right.

You say "hitt" for absolutely no reason, at the most random times, and it makes no sense and I love it anyway. You'll drop anything for a Splitsvilla episode. You're Guddi Papa and Yussu and Splitsvillian and meri jaan, all at once, and somehow all of those are exactly you.

So — happy belated birthday. Here's to nineteen being good to you, and to me getting to keep choosing you, one ordinary day at a time.

Hussainalways yours
